Aloo Paratha

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups wheat flour
  • 2 boiled potatoes
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp red chilli powder
  • 1 tbsp coriander leaves
  • Salt to taste
  • Ghee or oil for cooking

Steps:

  • Knead the wheat flour into a soft dough.
  • Grate and mix potatoes with spices.
  • Roll dough balls, stuff with potato mix, and seal.
  • Flatten into a round paratha.
  • Roast on tawa using ghee until golden brown.

Butter Chicken

Ingredients:

  • 400g boneless chicken (grilled)
  • 1 cup tomato puree
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1/2 cup cream
  • 1 tsp kasuri methi
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • Salt & spices as per taste

Steps:

  • Grill chicken pieces with spices.
  • Heat butter, add tomato puree and cook.
  • Add cream, kasuri methi, sugar, salt.
  • Add chicken and simmer for 10 mins.

Upma

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup sooji (semolina)
  • 1 chopped onion
  • 1 tsp mustard seeds
  • Curry leaves
  • Chopped vegetables (optional)
  • Salt & oil

Steps:

  • Heat oil, add mustard seeds, curry leaves, onion.

  • Add 2 cups water + salt and bring to boil.

White Sauce Pasta

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup pasta (boiled)
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 tbsp maida (flour)
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup cheese
  • Mixed herbs & black pepper

Steps:

  • Boil and drain pasta.
  • Heat butter, add maida and cook lightly.
  • Add milk slowly to avoid lumps.
  • Add cheese, herbs, salt & pepper.
  • Add boiled pasta, toss well and serve hot.

Chicken Biryani

Ingredients:

  • 500g chicken (marinated)
  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 1 onion (fried)
  • Yogurt + biryani masala
  • Mint & coriander leaves
  • Ghee, saffron milk, salt

Steps:

  • Boil rice 80%, drain.
  • Cook marinated chicken with masala and yogurt.
  • Layer rice + chicken + fried onions + mint + saffron milk.
  • Cover & cook on dum (low flame) for 20 mins.
